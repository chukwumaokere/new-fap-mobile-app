Dashboard Tiles:

  • Orders:
    • Orders Pending: Orders waiting for FAP approval
    • Orders In Process: Orders that are approved
    • Orders Received: Orders that are marked as Received
    • Orders Completed: All completed/fulfilled Orders
    • Orders Cancelled: Orders cancelled by FAP or by you
  • Payments:
    • Pending Invoices: Orders that require payment
    • Payments Past Due: Payments that are past their due date that you owe on
    • Last 30 Days: All payments records in the last 30 days
    • Payments Made: All payment records that are paid/closed in the last 90 days
  • Service Tickets:
    • Need Your Attention: Service tickets that need your input to move forward
    • New Tickets: Tickets you've recently created
    • Closed Tickets: All service tickets that are closed

Bottom Navigation:

  • Click to call FAP Support
  • Click to Go to Dashboard
  • Click to create a Service Ticket

    • Top Navigation:

  • Go back to the previous screen
  • Open the side navigaion
  • Refresh the current screen
